Why I Love Film Photography

My wonderful mom was the one who taught me film photography and it is a way that we both can connect and see the world. Here are three things as to why I am so passionate about film:

  1. Timeless Moments
    • It's a one shot only and the price of film makes every photo valuable.

  2. The Focus of the Memory
    • The photographer is not focused on perfection rather caputuring the memory as they can't instantly see what they took. It is an art of patience and hope.

  3. Tells a story
      4. Viewers can imagine the motion and story of each film photo. The colors of the film adds to the auroa of curiousity.
